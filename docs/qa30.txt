Does the monthly rate cover a single Azure plan or the entire account?
The flat monthly cost for Azure Support covers one account, regardless of how many subscriptions or users you have on the account. All subscriptions under an account will share the same support plan, and all users with admin/owner access to any of the subscriptions under the account with a Support Plan will also be entitled to support for those specific account’s subscriptions they have access to.
